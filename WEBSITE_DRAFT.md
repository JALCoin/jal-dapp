# JALSOL Website Draft

## Positioning

JALSOL should present as the public business domain of Jeremy Aaron Lugg: founder, builder, operator, and market-aware brand owner working in and around cryptocurrency, digital systems, and physical releases.

The site should feel:

- Precise
- Personal
- Market-aware
- Legible
- Accountable

The site should not feel:

- Anonymous
- Overhyped
- Meme-driven
- Excessively promotional
- Legally vague

## Brand Statement

Jeremy Aaron Lugg builds founder-led business systems, crypto-market aligned products, and brand-led physical releases through JALSOL.

## Homepage Goal

The homepage should answer five questions quickly:

1. Who is behind this site?
2. What does the business actually do?
3. How does crypto fit into the picture?
4. What can a visitor do here right now?
5. Why should the site be trusted?

## Visual Direction

### Dark Mode

Dark mode should be the primary mode.

Use:

- Deep charcoal or near-black base
- Controlled teal or emerald accent
- Warm gold highlight
- Muted grey-blue support tones

### Light Mode

Optional second phase.

If added later, it should use:

- Warm off-white background
- Dark graphite text
- Soft teal accents
- Low-glare gold highlights

Do not simply invert the dark mode palette.

## Interaction Direction

- Fast entry
- Minimal forced loading
- Clear hover states
- Motion used sparingly
- Reduced-motion support
